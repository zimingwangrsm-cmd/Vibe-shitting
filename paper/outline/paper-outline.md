# Lead Paper Outline / 首篇论文大纲

## Working Title / 工作标题

Read, Seen, Ignored: A Signaling-Game Analysis of Reply Latency in Advisor Group Chats  
已读、不回、稍后回复：导师群聊响应延迟的信号博弈分析

## Section Plan / 章节结构

1. Introduction / 引言  
   Why group-chat reply timing feels high-stakes in academic life.
2. Literature Review / 文献回顾  
   Position the work between signaling, organizational behavior, and digital etiquette.
3. Methods / 方法  
   Message annotation, delay buckets, hierarchy coding, payoff reconstruction.
4. Results / 结果  
   The optimal delay band, high-risk message categories, hierarchy effects.
5. Discussion / 讨论  
   Response latency as reputational labor.
6. Limitations / 局限  
   Platform-specific norms, cultural context, fake-data caution.
7. Conclusion / 结论  
   The student does not simply reply; the student performs reliability.
