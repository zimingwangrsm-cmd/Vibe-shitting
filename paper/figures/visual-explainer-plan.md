# Visual Explainer Plan / 插图与表情包辅助理解方案

## Principle / 原则

Use self-drawn diagrams as the primary visual layer, and treat third-party memes or screenshots as inspiration or optional appendix material.

以自绘图解作为主视觉层，把第三方表情包、截图和新闻图当作灵感来源或附录材料，而不是正文主图。

Reason:

- copyright and platform reuse are easier to manage
- the paper keeps a cleaner academic shell
- the humor still lands if the visual grammar is recognizable

## Recommended Insertions / 建议插图位置

### Slot A: before Section 4.1

**Purpose / 用途**  
Explain the new core concept, `Performative Presence Survivorship Bias / 体面在场幸存者偏差`.

**Best format / 最佳形式**  
`Reply Survivorship Plate`  
A self-drawn two-panel diagram with:

- the visible sample: public, timely, decorous replies
- the restored sample: delay, delegation, private handoff, strategic silence
- one arrow marking the inferential error
- one caption showing why the "normal" reply window looks earlier than it is

- 视觉逻辑可以借用经典“飞机幸存者偏差”图的思路，但必须完整自绘成项目自己的图版。

### Slot D: after Section 4.4

**Purpose / 用途**  
Explain why `first-author junior` and `procurement steward` have earlier response windows.

- high obligation / low veto
- high obligation / high veto

## Meme Handling Rule / 表情包使用规则

- If provenance is unclear, redraw.
- If the meme is highly platform-native, use it in social posts first, not in the core manuscript.
- The paper body should remain academically straight-faced.

如果原图来源不清楚，就重画。  
如果梗图过于平台原生，就先放到社媒帖里，不要直接塞进主稿正文。  
主稿正文仍然要保持“一本正经”的伪装。
