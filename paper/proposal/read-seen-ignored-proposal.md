# Research Proposal

## Title / 标题

**Read, Seen, Ignored: A Signaling-Game Analysis of Reply Latency in Advisor Group Chats**  
**已读、不回、稍后回复：导师群聊响应延迟的信号博弈分析**

## Project Summary / 项目摘要

This project studies an ordinary but high-stakes academic phenomenon: the timing of replies in advisor group chats.  
本项目研究一个日常但高压的学术现象：导师群聊中的回复时机。

The upgraded argument is that reply latency is not just etiquette. It is a hybrid of:

1. signaling under asymmetric information  
2. chronemic cueing in computer-mediated communication  
3. communication visibility before a mixed audience  
4. a multi-person volunteer's dilemma shaped by hierarchy and role ownership

升级后的核心论点是：回复时延并不只是礼貌问题，它同时是：

1. 不对称信息下的信号行为  
2. 计算机媒介沟通中的时序线索  
3. 在混合受众面前发生的可见性表演  
4. 受层级和任务归属影响的多人志愿者困境

The paper argues that graduate students do not optimize for immediate response. Instead, they search for a narrow delay band that balances four incompatible goals:

1. appearing diligent  
2. remaining reliable  
3. minimizing the probability of attracting new work  
4. avoiding public moral suspicion

本文认为，研究生优化的并不是“秒回”，而是一段狭窄的延迟区间，用来同时平衡四个互相冲突的目标：

1. 显得勤奋  
2. 保持可靠  
3. 尽量少接新活  
4. 避免在群里看起来“有问题”

## Theoretical Framework / 理论框架

### 1. Signaling and Reputation / 信号与声誉

Using Spence (1973) and Kreps & Wilson (1982), the paper treats reply latency as a repeated low-cost signal of diligence, deference, and available capacity.  
借助 Spence (1973) 与 Kreps & Wilson (1982)，本文把回复时延视为一种会反复出现的低成本信号，用来传达勤奋、服从与可用产能。

### 2. Chronemics in CMC / 计算机媒介沟通中的时间线索

Using Walther, Tidwell, Kalman, and Ziano & Wang, the paper treats timestamps as socioemotional cues rather than empty intervals.  
借助 Walther、Tidwell、Kalman 与 Ziano & Wang 的研究，本文将时间戳视为社会情绪线索，而不是空白时间。

### 3. Communication Visibility / 沟通可见性

Using Leonardi (2014), the project treats group chat latency as a public micro-performance observed by advisors, postdocs, seniors, and peers at once.  
借助 Leonardi (2014)，本项目把群聊回复理解为一种公开的微型表演，它同时被导师、博士后、学长学姐和同辈观察。

### 4. Volunteer's Dilemma + Expectation States / 志愿者困境 + 期望状态

Using Diekmann, Darley & Latané, and Berger et al., the project models group-chat delay as a strategic waiting game shaped by group size, role ownership, and hierarchy.  
借助 Diekmann、Darley & Latané 以及 Berger 等人的工作，本文将群聊延迟建模为一种由群体规模、任务归属和层级结构共同塑造的策略性等待博弈。

### 5. Vernacular Lab Politics / 实验室政治的民间语汇

As a satirical extension, the project incorporates folk role types drawn from Chinese platform discourse: the first-author junior, the procurement senior, the muddy-water fish, and MBTI-style duty-keeper archetypes. These are interpretive labels, not psychometric variables.  
作为讽刺性扩展，本文会吸收中文社交平台上的民间角色类型：一作学弟、负责采购的学长、浑水的鱼，以及 MBTI 式“值班型人格”等。这些只是解释性标签，不是心理测量变量。

## Research Questions / 研究问题

1. Does perceived diligence increase monotonically with reply speed, or does it follow an inverse-U pattern?  
   感知勤奋度是否随着回复速度单调上升，还是呈倒 U 型？
2. How do hierarchy and message visibility reshape the acceptable delay window?  
   层级关系和群聊可见性如何重塑可接受的延迟窗口？
3. How do group size, role ownership, and first visible reply change the strategic payoff of waiting?  
   群聊人数、任务归属和第一个可见回复，如何改变“先等等看”的策略收益？
4. Do holiday periods create a temporary rightward shift in acceptable latency?  
   假期是否会让“合理延迟”窗口暂时整体右移？
5. Can low-density emoji usage repair suspicion caused by delayed response?  
   低密度表情是否能修复延迟回复带来的怀疑？

## Hypotheses / 研究假设

### H1

Perceived diligence follows an inverse-U relation with latency. Extremely fast replies imply suspicious availability, while very late replies imply unreliability.  
感知勤奋度与延迟呈倒 U 型关系：过快显得“太闲”，过慢显得“不靠谱”。

### H2

Direct hierarchy compresses the optimal delay window. Messages from the principal advisor demand earlier response than messages from postdocs or peers.  
直接层级关系会压缩最优回复窗口：导师本人的消息比博士后或同门的消息要求更早。

### H3

Public visibility increases reputational penalty. When advisors and peers can all see the exchange, reply timing becomes more heavily interpreted as a signal of work ethic.  
公开可见性会提高声誉惩罚：当导师和同门都能看到对话时，回复时机会更强地被解读为工作态度信号。

### H4

Group size widens the initial waiting game only when ownership is ambiguous.  
只有在任务归属不明确时，群聊人数增多才会拉长最初的观望时间。

### H5

Ownership-bearing actors such as first authors, experiment operators, and procurement-reimbursement bottlenecks face earlier optimal windows than generic bystanders.  
一作、实验操作者和采购报销负责人等“背着活的人”，比普通围观者拥有更早的最优回复窗口。

### H6

Once a credible first reply appears, later replies lose much of their diligence premium and become primarily symbolic.  
一旦出现一个可信的首个回复，后续回复的“勤奋加分”会明显下降，更多变成象征性表态。

### H7

Holiday periods shift acceptable delay later for low- and medium-urgency messages, but they do not fully protect explicitly summoned actors.  
假期会让低、中紧急度消息的可接受延迟整体后移，但无法真正保护那些被明确点名的人。

### H8

Light emoji use can partially offset moderate delay for low-urgency messages, but fails under high-urgency or direct-task conditions.  
轻度表情使用可以部分抵消低紧急度消息中的中等延迟，但在高紧急度或直接任务情境下无效。

## Proposed Data Design / 数据设计

### Corpus / 语料

- 4,236 message-response dyads  
- 28 simulated or reconstructed research groups  
- group size ranging from 4 to 17  
- 173 student-side actors  
- observation window: January 2024 to December 2025

### Unit of Analysis / 分析单位

One visible group-chat message and the first visible student response that follows it.  
一条群消息及其之后第一个对外可见的学生回复。

### Core Variables / 核心变量

- latency bucket  
- sender hierarchy  
- group size  
- composition profile  
- message urgency  
- public visibility  
- task ownership  
- first visible reply / reply order  
- holiday phase  
- emoji density  
- perceived diligence score  
- assignment hazard  
- reputational survivability index

## Method / 方法

### 1. Message Annotation

Each interaction is coded by message type:

- hard summons  
- soft ping  
- paper comments  
- administrative reminder  
- reimbursement request  
- public praise trap  
- deadline escalation  
- holiday roll call

### 2. Group Structure Coding

Each event also records:

- who is visibly present in the group  
- whether the task clearly belongs to someone  
- whether another student has already replied  
- whether the interaction occurs immediately before or after a long holiday

### 3. Delay Bucketing

Response time is grouped into:

- `<2 min`  
- `2-7 min`  
- `8-18 min`  
- `19-47 min`  
- `48-180 min`  
- `>180 min`

### 4. Payoff Reconstruction

We model utility as:

`U_i(l_i) = alpha*D_i + beta*R_i - gamma*A_i - delta*B_i - eta*H_i`

where:

- `D` = perceived diligence  
- `R` = reliability  
- `A` = assignment hazard  
- `B` = blame or moral suspicion  
- `H` = holiday-degradation penalty

The key extension is that `A` and `B` depend on group size, ownership, and whether someone has already replied.  
这里的关键扩展是：`A` 和 `B` 都会受到群聊人数、任务归属以及是否已有他人先回复的影响。

## Expected Results / 预期结果

- The most stable routine zone will sit around 8 to 18 minutes.  
  对常规消息而言，最稳定的窗口预计会落在 8 到 18 分钟之间。
- Large groups will wait longer only when no one clearly owns the task.  
  只有在任务无人明确负责时，大群聊才会更长时间观望。
- First-author juniors and procurement seniors will face the harshest early-response pressure.  
  一作学弟和负责采购的学长，会承受最强的早回复压力。
- Once one credible person has replied, everyone else becomes less necessary.  
  一旦有一个可信的人先回复，其他人的“必要性”会明显下降。
- Holiday periods will add a temporary rightward shift without abolishing hierarchy.  
  假期会带来一个暂时右移的窗口，但不会废除层级。
