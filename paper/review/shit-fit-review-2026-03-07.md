## Why It Works Better Now / 为什么这版更像 SHIT

### 1. The title now lands immediately

`"Received, Professor"` / `“收到老师”` is a much better entrance than a generic signaling-game title. It feels lived, specific, and culturally sticky before the theory even begins.

### 2. The core bias is both rigorous and ridiculous

`Performative Presence Survivorship Bias` / `体面在场幸存者偏差` works because it imitates a real inference problem. It is not a random joke label. It actually explains why labs systematically overestimate how normal and manageable their group-chat order is.

### 3. The paper finally knows which jokes to keep

The draft now treats `first-author junior`, `procurement steward`, and `muddy-water fish` as disciplined role language rather than as a carnival of side jokes. That was the right cut.
