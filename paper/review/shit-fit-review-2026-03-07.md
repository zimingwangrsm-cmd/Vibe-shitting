## Strengths / 优点

### 1. The premise is strong

Reply latency in advisor group chats is a very good SHIT topic because it is:

- tiny in scale
- huge in emotional cost
- highly legible across labs, majors, and countries

导师群聊回复时延是一个非常好的 SHIT 题目，因为它：

- 事情很小  
- 心理代价很大  
- 跨专业、跨实验室、跨国家都能看懂

### 2. The theory stack is now real

The paper no longer relies on vibes alone. The combination of signaling, chronemics, communication visibility, expectation states, and volunteer's dilemma makes the joke feel structurally serious.

这篇稿子现在已经不只是靠“共鸣”撑着了。信号理论、时序线索、沟通可见性、期望状态和志愿者困境叠在一起，会让笑点显得非常“有结构”。

### 3. The role archetypes are SHIT-compatible

`first-author junior`, `procurement steward`, `muddy-water fish`, and `postdoc proxy` are strong figures because they are not random. They are miniature institutions.

“一作学弟”“采购学长”“浑水的鱼”“博士后代理人”这些角色非常合格，因为它们不是随机人设，而是微型制度。

## Main Findings / 主要问题

### Finding 1

The current draft is funnier in the literature review and discussion than in the results section.

现在这版稿子在文献综述和讨论部分比较好笑，但结果部分还偏稳，像是在认真汇报而不是在认真整活。

**Why it matters / 为什么重要**  
SHIT 最好的稿子，往往会让 “Results” 和 “Discussion” 也持续出笑点，而不是只在前言或结尾抖包袱。

**Fix direction / 修改方向**

- name the effects more aggressively
- add theorem-like corollaries
- let the tables themselves carry some jokes

建议命名：

- `Advisor-Visible Peer Compression Effect` / `导师在场同门压缩效应`
- `Default Volunteer Trap` / `默认接活人陷阱`
- `Atmospheric Reply Effect` / `氛围型回复效应`
- `Procurement Sovereignty Principle` / `采购主权原理`
- `Holiday Intelligence Drawdown` / `假期智力回撤`

### Finding 2

Some Chinese lab archetypes are funny to insiders but underexplained to outsiders.

有些中文语境里的实验室角色，对圈内人很好笑，但对圈外人解释还不够。

**Examples / 例子**

- `一作学弟`: high manuscript ownership, low institutional power
- `采购学长`: low symbolic glory, high administrative leverage
- `浑水的鱼`: a rational bystander who waits for someone more legible to reply first

**Fix direction / 修改方向**

- add one-sentence glosses the first time each role appears
- pair each role with a visual plate or icon

### Finding 3

The visual layer is still thinner than the conceptual layer.

视觉层还没有跟上概念层。

The paper now has enough models and named effects to support a richer image system:

- theory framework map
- first-responder discount chart
- role-obligation matrix
- holiday-fog shift diagram
- meme-assisted appendix or social supplement

### Finding 4

The paper needs 2-4 more outrageous conclusions.

这篇稿子还需要 2-4 个更“逆天”的结论，作为 SHIT 味道的收口。

Recommended corollaries:

1. The most exploitable student is not the most competent student, but the most consistently well-timed one.  
   最容易被持续加活的学生，不是最能干的学生，而是回复时机最稳定的学生。
2. In sufficiently large groups, collective intelligence decreases until a single person is explicitly named.  
   在足够大的群聊里，群体智能会持续下降，直到有人被明确点名。
3. A laboratory can reduce visible panic without reducing workload simply by redistributing who replies first.  
   一个实验室即使不减少任何工作量，也可以仅通过重新分配“谁先回消息”来降低可见恐慌。
4. After long holidays, the lab's cognitive recovery curve lags behind the advisor's scheduling curve by at least one meeting cycle.  
   长假之后，实验室群体认知恢复曲线通常至少落后于导师排会曲线一个会议周期。
