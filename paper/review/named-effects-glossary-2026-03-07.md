# Named Effects and Folk Roles / 效应与民间角色词表

This glossary now follows the older manuscript's flavor-first direction.  
这个词表现在跟随旧稿的“味道优先”方向。

## Named Effects / 主要效应

### Call-Out Effect / 点名效应

- Definition: once the advisor explicitly names or `@` mentions a student, the safe delay window collapses and most group-level camouflage stops working.
- 中文定义：导师一旦直接点名或 `@` 某个人，这个人的安全延迟窗口会立刻塌缩，群体掩护基本失效。

### First-Responder Discount / 首回复折扣

- Definition: once one credible public reply appears, later replies lose much of their marginal diligence value.
- 中文定义：一旦群里已经出现一个可信的公开回复，后续回复的边际勤奋收益会迅速缩水。

### Advisor Red-Envelope Effect / 导师红包效应

- Definition: a tiny public red envelope from the advisor revives dormant members much faster than ordinary supervision.
- 中文定义：导师发一个很小的公开红包，也能比普通监督更快唤醒潜水成员。

## Folk Species / 民间物种

### Gatekeeping Dog / 看门的狗

- The person who replies fast, forwards notices, guards process, and behaves as if order itself must be defended.
- 那个回得快、转通知、盯流程、仿佛秩序本身都要靠他来守的人。

### Muddy-Water Fish / 浑水的鱼

- The actor who survives by waiting for a more status-legible organism to move first.
- 靠“等更像责任人的人先动”来生存的那个角色。

### Dragging Bottle / 拖油的瓶

- The actor whose own slow tempo drags the visible rhythm of the shared task chain.
- 自己的节奏一慢，就会把整条任务链条都拖慢的人。

### First-Author Junior / 一作学弟

- High manuscript ownership, low veto power.
- 稿件归他，否决权不归他。

### Procurement Steward / 采购学长

- Low symbolic glory, high invoice sovereignty.
- 荣光不一定归他，但发票和耗材经常归他。

## Structural Note / 结构说明

- The manuscript no longer relies on a grand umbrella concept.
- The paper now works through a small set of crisp effects plus vivid role types.
- 这版稿子不再依赖一个大而空的总概念。
- 现在的结构是：几个利落效应，加一组很有画面的角色分类。
