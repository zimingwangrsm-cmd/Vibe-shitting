# Core Concepts Glossary / 核心概念词表

This glossary intentionally shrinks the manuscript's term system.  
这个词表有意缩减全文的命名体系。

The paper now runs on **one core bias**, **three mechanisms**, and **two moderators**.  
现在整篇稿子的结构是：**一个主偏差**、**三个机制**、**两个调节条件**。

## 1. Core Bias / 主偏差

### Performative Presence Survivorship Bias / 体面在场幸存者偏差

- Definition: inferring the "normal" rhythm of advisor-group-chat life from the subset of people who remain publicly visible through timely and decorous replies.
- 中文定义：只从那些仍能在公开场合及时、体面回复的人身上推断导师群聊的正常节奏。
- Validation: compare a visible-reply-only model with a full-opportunity model that restores delayed, delegated, private, and missing outcomes.
- 验证方式：对比“只看公开回复”的可见样本模型与“把延迟、代理、私聊和缺席加回去”的全机会模型。

## 2. Main Mechanisms / 三个核心机制

### Advisor-Visible Compression Effect / 导师在场压缩效应

- Definition: peer messages behave more like supervision when the advisor is visibly present.
- 中文定义：只要导师在看，同门消息的时间结构就会向监督场景压缩。

### Ownership Revelation Mechanism / 责任显影机制

- Definition: once task ownership becomes legible, waiting stops being neutral and quickly turns into blame exposure.
- 中文定义：任务归属一旦显影，继续观望就不再中立，而会迅速变成责备风险。
- Included subpatterns / 包含子现象:
  - `Default Volunteer Trap / 默认接活人陷阱`
  - `First-Responder Discount / 首回复折损`

### Red-Envelope Resurrection Effect / 红包复活效应

- Definition: tiny public gifts from advisors generate a disproportionately large return of gratitude, attendance, and visible presence.
- 中文定义：导师发一个很小的公开红包，也能触发远大于金额本身的在场回流。

## 3. Moderators / 调节条件

### Role Heterogeneity / 角色异质性

- The first-author junior, procurement steward, experiment operator, postdoc proxy, and muddy-water fish do not share the same timing obligation.
- 一作学弟、采购学长、实验操作者、博士后代理人和浑水的鱼，不在同一条时间义务曲线上。

### Holiday Fog / 假期脑雾

- Low-urgency tolerance moves rightward after holidays, but hierarchy barely relaxes.
- 节后低紧急度窗口会右移，但层级几乎不会真正放松。

## 4. Deprecated or Demoted Terms / 已降级术语

- `Procurement Sovereignty Principle / 采购主权原理`
  Used now as a role explanation rather than a headline mechanism.
  现在保留为角色解释，不再单独作为主机制标题。

- `Holiday Intelligence Drawdown / 假期智力回撤`
  Folded into the holiday-fog moderator to keep the paper cleaner.
  现并入“假期脑雾”调节层，以减少名词堆积。

- `Advisor Red-Envelope Effect / 导师发红包效应`
  Renamed to `Red-Envelope Resurrection Effect / 红包复活效应` for sharper imagery.
  现改名为“红包复活效应”，画面感更强。
