# Named Effects Glossary / 命名效应词表

## Already Integrated / 已经入稿

### 1. Advisor-Visible Peer Compression Effect / 导师在场同门压缩效应

When the advisor is visibly present, even peer-originated messages behave like semi-supervisory messages.
只要导师在场，同门发来的消息也会在时间结构上半垂直化。

### 2. Default Volunteer Trap / 默认接活人陷阱

Once ownership becomes obvious, somebody can no longer hide in the group's ambiguity.
任务归属一旦清晰，总会有人被系统推出水面。

### 3. First-Responder Discount / 首回复折扣

Once one acceptable reply appears, later replies become increasingly ceremonial.
一旦已经有人体面地先回了，后续回复的边际加分会迅速下降。

### 4. Procurement Sovereignty Principle / 采购主权原理

The least celebrated person may still control the most operationally important resources.
最不显眼的人，未必不是掌握实际权力的人。

### 5. Holiday Intelligence Drawdown / 假期智力回撤

After a long holiday, cognitive recovery lags behind institutional scheduling recovery.
长假后人的思维恢复，往往慢于机构的排会恢复。

### 6. Advisor Red-Envelope Effect / 导师发红包效应

A tiny public gift can revive dormant members faster than ordinary supervision.
一个很小的公开红包，也能比普通监督更快地唤醒潜水成员。

## Candidate Terms / 可继续扩写的候选名词

### 7. Atmospheric Reply Effect / 氛围型回复效应

Replies posted after the situation is already socially stabilized contribute mood more than meaning.
局势已经稳定之后发出的回复，更多是在补气氛，而不是补信息。

### 8. Read-Receipt Camouflage Effect / 已读伪装效应

Someone reads the message immediately but delays the visible reply to preserve plausible busyness.
一个人明明已经看见消息，却故意晚点回，以维持“合理忙碌”的外观。

### 9. Screenshot Deterrence Effect / 截图威慑效应

People reply more carefully when any message may later become portable evidence.
当任何一句话都可能被截图带走时，回复会自动变得更加谨慎。

### 10. Proxy Thunder Effect / 代理人雷鸣效应

A postdoc or senior relaying the advisor's intention often produces nearly the same fear response as the advisor's direct speech.
博士后或学长代传导师意志时，往往会引发接近导师本人发话的紧张反应。

### 11. Gratitude Inflation Effect / 感谢膨胀效应

Under visible hierarchy, a minor favor can generate disproportionate public thanks.
在可见层级场景中，一个很小的善意也会膨胀成过度公开的感谢表演。

### 12. Meeting-Eve Resurrection Effect / 会前复活效应

Members who remain silent for hours may suddenly regain language shortly before a scheduled meeting.
长时间沉默的群成员，常常会在会议开始前短暂恢复语言能力。

### 13. Reagent Oracle Effect / 试剂神谕效应

The person who controls ordering and arrival updates can temporarily become the lab's most authoritative voice.
掌握采购和到货信息的人，会在某些时刻短暂成为实验室里最像权威的人。

### 14. Silent Attendance Tax / 沉默出勤税

Failing to visibly respond in important collective moments accumulates a reputational cost, even when no explicit task is assigned.
在某些重要公共时刻没有可见回应，即便没人明确点活，也会累计一种声誉税。
