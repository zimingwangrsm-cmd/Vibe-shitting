# Vernacular Scenes and Meme Notes / 民间场景与梗图笔记

This note records the public, crawlable context materials used to sharpen the manuscript's scene language.

## Search conclusion

- Exact Xiaohongshu or Weibo origins for role labels such as `看门的狗`, `浑水的鱼`, and `拖油的瓶` were not stably indexable from this environment.
- They are therefore treated as field slang and synthesized role tags, not as directly attributed quotes from a single source post.
- Publicly crawlable meme pages and advice articles were easier to verify than platform-native comment threads.

## Writing implication

- Keep the paper body academically straight-faced.
- Put direct meme references in notes, figures, or social assets instead of in the main evidence chain.
- Treat role labels as vernacular sociology: vivid enough to be funny, disciplined enough to remain legible.
