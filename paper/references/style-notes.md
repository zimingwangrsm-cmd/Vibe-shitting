# Style Notes / 风格备忘

## What To Emulate / 要学什么

- deadpan seriousness
- recognizable real-life pain
- formal structure
- one or two convincing visuals

## What To Avoid / 不要学什么

- random profanity
- pure ranting
- real harmful instructions
- topics that depend on insider knowledge only

## SHIT Modes / SHIT 的三种稳定模式

1. Absurd premise + rigorous method  
2. Everyday shame + productivity science  
3. Collective emotional pain + social-science framing
