# Phase 01 - SHIT 风格研究 / SHIT Style Study

## Goal / 目标

Understand what makes SHIT funny enough to imitate without collapsing into low-effort parody.

## Findings / 关键结论

SHIT 的幽默主要有三条路数：

1. **荒谬问题 + 严格方法**  
   例如：蜂医角色与男性盆底健康、地府货币膨胀。
2. **日常羞耻 + 生产力科学**  
   例如：如厕时间与生产力上升。
3. **真实情绪 + 社会科学包装**  
   例如：东亚家庭“恨海情天”。

## Tone Rules / 语气规则

- the premise may be absurd, but the method must look serious
- the paper should hit a real feeling or shared experience
- the humor should be deadpan, not noisy

## Safety Rules / 安全边界

- no real politics
- no dangerous crime or medical guidance
- no doxxing or real-person harm

## Output / 本阶段输出

- style notes
- source summary
- candidate topics
- a ready-to-share social summary
